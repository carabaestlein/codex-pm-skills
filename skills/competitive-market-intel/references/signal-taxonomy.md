# Signal Taxonomy

Classify each item using one primary signal type. Add a secondary type only when it genuinely helps.

## Product

- Launches
- Feature expansions
- UX changes
- Integrations
- Platform/API changes
- Deprecations

## Packaging And Pricing

- Public pricing changes
- Plan or entitlement changes
- Free trial changes
- Usage limits
- Enterprise packaging
- Discounting or procurement signals

## Positioning

- Messaging changes
- ICP shifts
- Category creation or repositioning
- New comparison pages
- New claims about AI, security, compliance, scale, or ROI

## Go-To-Market

- Partnerships
- Channel programs
- Marketplace motion
- Geographic expansion
- Events and campaigns
- Customer proof

## Company And Market

- Funding
- M&A
- Executive changes
- Hiring patterns
- Regulatory changes
- Analyst or market narrative shifts

## Customer Sentiment

- Review themes
- Social complaints or praise
- Community discussions
- Support or reliability patterns

## Prioritization Heuristics

Score signals higher when they:

- Affect your ICP or core buying criteria.
- Change the competitor's product promise or packaging.
- Suggest a new wedge, moat, or sales objection.
- Are primary-source confirmed.
- Repeat across multiple sources.

Score signals lower when they are routine content marketing, undated, generic, or disconnected from PM decisions.
