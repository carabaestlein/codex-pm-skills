# Digest Format

Use this default format unless the user requests otherwise.

```markdown
Subject: Morning Market Intel: <3-5 word summary>

## Executive Summary

<2-4 bullets summarizing the most important changes and what they may mean.>

## Top Signals

### 1. <Competitor or market actor>: <what changed>

- **Signal type:** <taxonomy label>
- **What changed:** <factual summary>
- **Why it matters:** <PM interpretation>
- **PM implication:** <specific product, packaging, positioning, or research implication>
- **Confidence:** <High | Medium | Low>
- **Sources:** <dated links>

## Watchlist

- <Lower-confidence signal or thing to monitor next.>

## Suggested Follow-Ups

- <Specific action, owner type, or research question.>

## Sources

- <Source title> - <URL>
```

## Style

- Direct and concise.
- No hype.
- No long preamble.
- Use bullets for scanability.
- Keep each top signal to roughly 100-180 words unless the user asks for a deeper memo.
